Getting Started
1. Register as a Miner

Go to the registration page and provide your username and GXC wallet address.

2. Configure Your Miner

Use the following Stratum URL in your mining software:

stratum+tcp://{{ pool_name }}.gxc.network:{{ pool_port }}
3. Start Mining

Once connected, your miner will start submitting shares and earning rewards.
