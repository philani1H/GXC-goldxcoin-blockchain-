Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Miner username and wallet address
  • Mining statistics (hash rate, shares, etc.)
  • Connection logs and timestamps
2. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Calculate and distribute mining rewards
  • Maintain pool statistics and leaderboards
  • Improve pool services
3. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law.

5. Public Information

Mining statistics and leaderboard information may be publicly displayed on the pool website.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, please contact the pool administrator.
