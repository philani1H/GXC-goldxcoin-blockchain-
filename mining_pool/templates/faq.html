Frequently Asked Questions

A mining pool is a group of miners who combine their computational resources to increase their chances of finding blocks and earning rewards. Rewards are distributed among pool members based on their contribution.

Register on the registration page with your username and GXC wallet address. Then configure your miner software to connect to the pool using the Stratum URL provided.

The pool fee is typically 1% of your earnings. This fee covers pool operation costs and maintenance.

Payouts are processed daily for miners who have reached the minimum payout threshold (0.1 GXC). Payments are sent directly to your registered wallet address.

You can use any mining software that supports the {{ algorithm|upper }} algorithm and Stratum protocol. Popular options include CGMiner, BFGMiner, and other compatible miners.

Your share of pool rewards is calculated based on the number of valid shares you submit relative to the total shares submitted by all miners in the pool. The more shares you contribute, the larger your portion of the block reward.
