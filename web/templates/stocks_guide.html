Stock Contracts Guide

Complete guide to tokenized stock contracts on GXC blockchain

What are Stock Contracts?

Stock Contracts on GXC blockchain are tokenized representations of real-world stocks that enable trading, ownership tracking, and corporate actions on-chain.

Real-World Assets

Each contract represents shares of a real company (AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL, etc.) with 1:1 backing and real-time price feeds.

Real-Time Prices

Prices updated via Proof-of-Price (PoP) oracle network with data from Yahoo Finance and other trusted sources.

Full Traceability

Every share transfer is chained and traceable, ensuring complete auditability and compliance.

Corporate Actions

Support for dividends, stock splits, mergers, and other corporate actions with on-chain execution.

How Stock Contracts Work

1
Contract Deployment

Stock contracts are deployed on the GXC blockchain with:

  • Ticker Symbol: Stock ticker (e.g., AAPL, MSFT)
  • Company Name: Full company name
  • Exchange: Stock exchange (NYSE, NASDAQ, etc.)
  • Price Oracle: Authorized price feed source
2
Price Updates

Real-time price updates via Proof-of-Price (PoP) oracle network:

// Price update with PoP hash stockContract.updatePrice( newPrice: 175.50, timestamp: current_time, popHash: "0xabc123...", source: "yahoo_finance" )
3
Share Issuance & Trading

Shares can be issued, transferred, and traded on-chain:

// Issue shares to an address stockContract.issueShares(to: "GXC1...", shares: 100) // Transfer shares between addresses stockContract.transferShares( from: "GXC1...", to: "GXC2...", shares: 50 ) // Check balance balance = stockContract.balanceOf("GXC1...")
4
Corporate Actions

Support for dividends, splits, mergers, and other corporate actions:

  • Dividends: Automatic distribution to shareholders
  • Stock Splits: Automatic share adjustment
  • Mergers: Exchange ratio-based conversions
  • Voting: Shareholder voting on proposals

Key Features

Real-Time Price Feeds

Prices updated every minute from Yahoo Finance and other trusted sources. All price updates include Proof-of-Price (PoP) hashes for verification.

Transaction Chaining

Every share transfer references the previous transaction, creating an unbreakable chain of ownership history for complete traceability.

Dividend Distribution

Automatic dividend payments to shareholders based on their holdings. Dividends are distributed in GXC tokens.

Stock Splits & Mergers

Automatic handling of stock splits (e.g., 2:1 split doubles all holdings) and mergers with exchange ratio calculations.

KYC & Compliance

Optional KYC verification and transfer restrictions for regulatory compliance. Trading can be halted if needed.

Shareholder Management

Track all shareholders, their balances, and voting power. Complete transfer history for each address.

Corporate Actions

Stock contracts support various corporate actions that are executed on-chain:

Dividends

Automatic dividend distribution to all shareholders based on their holdings. Dividends are paid in GXC tokens.

// Distribute dividend stockContract.distributeDividend( dividendPerShare: 0.25, proofHash: "0x..." ) // Claim dividend stockContract.claimDividend("GXC1ADDRESS")
Stock Splits

Automatic share adjustment when a stock split occurs. For example, a 2:1 split doubles all shareholder balances.

// Execute 2:1 stock split stockContract.executeSplit(ratio: 2.0) // All balances automatically doubled // 100 shares → 200 shares
Mergers

Handle mergers with exchange ratio calculations. Shares are converted based on the merger terms.

// Execute merger stockContract.executeMerger( exchangeRatio: 0.5, targetContract: "TARGET_TICKER" ) // 100 shares → 50 shares of target
Voting

Shareholders can vote on corporate action proposals. Voting power is proportional to share holdings.

// Propose corporate action actionId = stockContract.proposeCorporateAction( actionType: DIVIDEND, value: 0.25, description: "Q1 2024 Dividend" ) // Vote on proposal stockContract.voteCorporateAction( actionId: actionId, voter: "GXC1ADDRESS", approve: true )
